=========================================================================
SECTION 6 — ATTRIBUTION AND INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
=========================================================================

The Author retains all intellectual property rights in the Software,
including the following novel contributions:

  1. The fixed-phi12 TPS modulation strategy creating a monotone
     Pref-to-phi3 mapping (r=0.994) enabling reliable Transformer
     learning of DAB modulation angles.

  2. The calibrated power model P=K*(phi12/pi)*phi3*(1-phi3/B) fitted
     from numerical simulation for high-power DAB converters.

  3. The VideoWaveformExtractor pipeline for extracting TPS modulation
     parameters from oscilloscope or simulation screen-recording videos.

  4. The combined synthetic-plus-video training architecture merging
     simulation-generated labels with real hardware measurements.

  5. The four-mode embedded deployment controller architecture with
     outer PI and inner PITNN control separated into importable files.
