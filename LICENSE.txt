INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
=========================================================================

The Author retains all intellectual property rights in the Software,
including rights to the following novel contributions:

  - The fixed-phi12 TPS modulation strategy that creates a monotone
    Pref-to-phi3 mapping (r = 0.994) enabling reliable Transformer
    learning.

  - The calibrated power model P = K*(phi12/pi)*phi3*(1-phi3/B) with
    constants fitted from numerical simulation for high-power DAB
    converters.

  - The VideoWaveformExtractor pipeline for extracting TPS modulation
    parameters from oscilloscope and simulation screen-recording videos
    without code modification.

  - The combined synthetic-plus-video training architecture that merges
    simulation-generated labels with real hardware measurements.

  - The deployment architecture separating training (pitnn_dab.py) from
    inference (pitnn_deploy.py) with a four-mode embedded deployment
    controller.
